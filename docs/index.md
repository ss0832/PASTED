# PASTED

**P**ointless **A**tom **ST**ructure with **E**ntropy **D**iagnostics

A structure fuzzer for quantum chemistry (QC) and machine-learning potential
(MLP) codes.  Generates intentionally random, physically meaningless atomic
structures and quantifies their disorder through 13 structural metrics.
